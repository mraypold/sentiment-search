Enter a search term to search twitter. A list of up to five tweets will be returned, labelled based on sentiment.

A good place to start would be with :) or :(

The algorithm is good at picking up extreme sentiments, but falls short on more neutral weighted tweets.

Sensitive media is not filtered, so be careful with your search terms!
